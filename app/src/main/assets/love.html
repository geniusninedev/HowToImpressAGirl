Reasons Why I Love You

Have you ever thought why you love your partner so much? More than you, it's your partner who would love to know the reasons why you love him/her. Express your deepest feelings, especially when you’re in a relationship or when someone really captures your heart.

Creating a list of “Top 10 Reasons Why I Love You” is a wonderful way to celebrate togetherness and let your love know what he/she means to you.

You might feel a deluge of emotions while writing the reasons. No matter how overwhelming the idea is to write top 10 reasons why I love you, it is ultimately the cute gesture that will bring smile on your sweetie’s face.

Tips to Help You Compose Your List

 Pick out specifics about your sweetheart. The more unique, the better.

Go into detail about why – it’s better to have three reasons you love him or her with a solid explanation than fifty without one at all.

This is a love letter by another name, so take care in composing it. Rushing is a major mistake.

On the other hand, do not get so wrapped up in choosing the perfect words that it’s never completed. Make a first draft and edit it, then pass it along.

Don’t ever lie. If you are just going to fake it, you’re better off not making a list.

Now that you’ve got those pointers in mind, here is some inspiration on what to choose when the time comes to share the reasons why you love your dearest…

Best Reasons Why I Love You

“The way you ensure I always have what I need to get over a bad day makes me love you more and more all the time.”

“I would not be able to be the person I am today if you were not in my life.”

“I am continually amazed by the depths of your kindness and your willingness to care for others, not just me.”

“If ever there were a reason to love someone, the support you offer for my hopes and dreams would have to be one of the best.”

“I love the way you hold me close at the end of a long week. It makes the wait to leave work nearly unbearable.”

“When I’m with you, it seems like we have the kind of love old movies were written about. It’s almost like we’ve known each other our whole lives.”

“Your sense of humor is the reason so many people love your company – and a big part of why I love you so much.”

“I don’t know why anyone else falls in love, but the fact I feel safe and secure when I’m around you is a major reason for me.”

“From the moment I met you, I could tell there was something special about you. I think the gleam in your eye made me love you from the start.”

“I have never met a person so strong in their convictions, so able to resist the low road in favor of higher things – and that’s one of the major reasons why I love you.”
