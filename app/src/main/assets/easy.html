Easy Ways to Impress a Girl

1. Look Her in the Eye while Talking

This is the first tip in our list of 10 easy ways to impress a girl.When you talk to her, try to look in to her eyes most of the time. Not in a creepy way like you are staring but in a more confidant and nice way. She won’t say anything but will like it.

2. Be Well Groomed

Facial hair, long and creepy hair, untidy dress, mismatched clothing or anything that shows you don’t care about yourself decrease your chances. Girls like if you show that you take care yourself when it comes to grooming. You don’t have to be clean shaved all the time but at least well maintained facial hair. This is one of the most important tips in our list of 10 easy ways to impress a girl.

4. Ignore Your Phone

If you are looking for 10 easy ways to impress a girl then this is the easiest of all, Ignore your phone while you are with her. Do it at least when you are with a girl whom you want to impress. Let her know that even your phone is not important more than her.

5. Ask Her Questions

Women do like to talk a lot but also like when you ask some personal questions, like where were you born or brought up. Ask her about some college or school stuff. (Don’t ask very personal questions)

6. Open Doors

Another one in our list of 10 easy ways to impress a girl. Simple but effective way to impress a girl. Do small things like opening door for her, pulling chair etc.

7. Compliment Her

You have to. And no need to give very serious compliment always like beautiful eyes and blah blah. You can compliment her by saying how good that dress looks on her, or her fashion taste is good and things like that. This is one of the easy ways to impress a girl.

8. Ask For Advice

Include her in your simple decisions like what to buy for gift, or which apartment will be better or how is that restaurant. Girls love to be asked for advice and also shows that you value others opinion.

9. Don’t Smoke

Doesn’t matter how much you feel that smoking is cool but it has negative effect on girls. In survey conducted by match.com 9 out of 10 girl don’t want to go out with men who smokes. So if you are looking to impress a girl then you have to drop the idea of smoking.

10. Have a Sense of Humour

No big surprise here. Women like men who are funny and have a sense of humour. But do remember not to over do the jokes and become creepy. Not every one is Chandler Bing who can make jokes any time anywhere.
